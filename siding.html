Siding & Gutters

Increase your home's visual appeal!

At Family Home Improvement (FHI), we offer comprehensive siding services designed to enhance your home’s appearance, protection, and energy efficiency. Our team specializes in all types of siding, including vinyl, shake, Hardie board, metal, and shingle siding, giving you a wide selection of materials to match your home’s style and durability needs.

We handle full siding installations, repairs, and replacements, using only high-quality materials and a precise installation process to ensure a seamless, weather-resistant finish that’s built to last. In addition to siding, we provide gutter replacement services to enhance home’s drainage and protection. Our expertise also extends to windows; we not only install new windows but also apply the proper siding border around them, ensuring both aesthetic appeal and structural integrity.

With FHI, you can trust each project—whether siding, gutters, or windows—will be completed with professionalism and attention to detail. Quality siding not only improves curb appeal but also adds insulation and protection against the elements, making it a valuable investment in your home’s performance and longevity.

Siding Services

We specialize in a wide variety of siding types, including vinyl, shake, Hardie board, metal, and shingle siding, allowing you to choose the perfect look and durability for your home. High-quality siding is essential for protecting your property, enhancing curb appeal, and adding energy efficiency by improving insulation. Our detailed process includes removing old siding, replacing any weakened plywood, and installing insulation to ensure your home stays comfortable and efficient year-round. We also handle all necessary installations for electrical and light boxes, ensuring a smooth, seamless fit with your new siding. With Family Home Improvement LLC, you can trust that every step of the process is carried out with care and precision to provide lasting quality and value.

Gutter Services

We understand that a reliable gutter system is key to protecting your home from water damage and maintaining the integrity of your siding and foundation. Proper gutter installation should be handled by skilled workers experienced in siding services, as it requires careful positioning and fitting. We offer comprehensive gutter replacements and repositioning services to ensure that your gutters are optimized for effective drainage and durability.
