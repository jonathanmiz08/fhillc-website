Roofing

Quality Roofing Services with 30 years guaranteed!

At Family Home Improvement (FHI), we are dedicated to delivering exceptional roofing services that prioritize quality, reliability, and speed. Specializing in both shingle and flat roofing, our team is committed to providing long-lasting solutions for all your roofing needs. We use only the best materials ensuring a durable and visually-appealing finish. Our partnerships with prompt and reliable suppliers guarantee that projects remain on schedule.

With extensive experience in roof valleys, complex angles, and intricate roofing details, we have the expertise to handle any challenges that come our way. Our comprehensive services include full installations, roof rip-offs, and targeted repairs to ensure that every aspect of your roof is in perfect condition. We go the extra mile by replacing damaged or weakened plywood to reinforce the structural integrity of your roof and applying an ice and water shield for maximum weather protection.

Trust FHI to keep your home or business safe and secure with roofing solutions that stand the test of time, backed by our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Shingle Roofing

Our shingle roofing services focus on delivering durable and high-quality results using top-grade materials. We primarily work with GAF shingles, known for their reliability and longevity, but we are happy to accommodate different brands upon customer request. Our comprehensive process for replacing old roofs includes a full rip-off of existing shingles, followed by the replacement of any weakened or damaged plywood to reinforce the roof’s structure. For both new and existing roofs, we install high-quality underlayment paper, as well as ice and water shield along roof edges and valleys for enhanced weather protection, creating a durable and weather-resistant barrier beneath the new roof shingles. Our team is also skilled in replacing chimney flashing and pipe boots, and performing necessary repairs to ensure a seamless fit that prevents leaks and extends the life of your roof. If you’re looking for a new roof or need repairs to keep your home secure and well-protected for the next 30 years, we are here to help. Contact Family Home Improvement LLC today for a reliable and professional roofing service that prioritizes your home’s safety and value.
